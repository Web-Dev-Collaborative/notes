**NAME**  
ln &#8211; make links between files  
**SYNOPSYS**  
`ln [OPTION]... [-T] TARGET LINK_NAME<br />
ln [OPTION]... TARGET<br />
ln [OPTION]... TARGET... DIRECTORY<br />
ln [OPTION]... -t DIRECTORY TARGET...`  
**EXAMPLE**  
`ln -s /path/source /path/filename`
