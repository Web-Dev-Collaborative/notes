**NAME**  
sed &#8211; stream editor for filtering and transforming text  
**SYNOPSYS**  
`sed [OPTION]... {script-only-if-no-other-script} [input-file]...`  
**EXAMPLE**  
`sed 's/foo/bar/g' file`
