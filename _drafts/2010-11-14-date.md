**NAME**  
date &#8211; print or set the system date and time  
**SYNOPSYS**  
`date [OPTION]... [+FORMAT]<br />
date [-u|--utc|--universal] [MMDDhhmm[[CC]YY][.ss]]`  
**EXAMPLE**  
`date --set 24:00:0`
