**NAME**  
`rsh - remote shell<br />
<strong>SYNOPSIS</strong><br />
rsh host [-l username] [-n] [-d] [-k realm] [-f | -F] [-x] [-PN | -PO] command<br />
rsh [-Kdnx] [-k realm] [-l username] host<br />
rsh [-Kdnx] [-k realm] username@host [command]`
