**NAME**  
mail, mailx &#8211; send and receive mail  
**SYNOPSYS**  
`mail [-eIinv] [-a header] [-b bcc-addr] [-c cc-addr] [-s subject] to-addr           ... [-- sendmail-options ...]<br />
mail [-eIiNnv] -f [name]<br />
mail [-eIiNnv] [-u user]`  
**EXAMPLE**  
`cat mailfile | mail -s "subject" "email"`
