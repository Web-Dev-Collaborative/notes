**NAME**  
kill &#8211; send a signal to a process  
**SYNOPSYS**  
`kill [ -signal | -s signal ] pid ...<br />
kill [ -L | -V, --version ]<br />
kill -l  [ signal ]`  
**EXAMPLE**  
`kill -9 pidnumber`
