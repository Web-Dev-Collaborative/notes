**NAME**  
chown &#8211; change file owner and group  
**SYNOPSYS**  
`chown [OPTION]... [OWNER][:[GROUP]] FILE...<br />
 chown [OPTION]... --reference=RFILE FILE...`  
**EXAMPLE**  
`chown user:group_user file<br />
chown -R user:group_user directory`
