**NAME**  
cp &#8211; copy files and directories **SYNOPSYS**  
`cp [OPTION]... [-T] SOURCE DEST<br />
cp [OPTION]... SOURCE... DIRECTORY<br />
cp [OPTION]... -t DIRECTORY SOURCE...`  
**EXAMPLE**  
`cp -rf directory1 directory2`
