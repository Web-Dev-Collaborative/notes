**NAME**  
chmod &#8211; change file mode bits 

**SYNOPSYS**  
`chmod [OPTION]... MODE[,MODE]... FILE...<br />
chmod [OPTION]... OCTAL-MODE FILE...<br />
chmod [OPTION]... --reference=RFILE FILE...`  
**EXAMPLE**  
`chmod 644 file<br />
chmod -R 644 directory`
