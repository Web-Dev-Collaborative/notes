**NAME**  
grep, egrep, fgrep, rgrep &#8211; print lines matching a pattern  
**SYNOPSYS**  
`grep [OPTIONS] PATTERN [FILE...]<br />
grep [OPTIONS] [-e PATTERN | -f FILE] [FILE...]`  
**EXAMPLE**  
`grep -R foo directory<br />
grep -c foo file<br />
egrep ".*foo" file`
