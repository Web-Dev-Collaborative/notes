nc &#8211; TCP/IP swiss army knife

**SYNOPSIS**  
> `nc [-options] hostname port[s] [ports] ...`<br />
`nc -l -p port [-options] [hostname] [port]`

**EXAMPLE**  
> `nc -l -p 2345`
