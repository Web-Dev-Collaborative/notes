**NAME**  
nmap &#8211; Network exploration tool and security / port scanner  
**SYNOPSIS**  
> `nmap [Scan Type...] [Options] {target specification}`

**EXAMPLE**  
> `nmap -sT -p 22,3306 IP.SE.RV.ER`<br />
`nmap -sS -O IP.SE.RV.ER`
