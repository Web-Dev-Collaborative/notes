**NAME**  
lighttpd &#8211; a fast, secure and flexible web server  
**SYNOPSIS**  
> `lighttpd [-ptDvVh] -f configfile [-m moduledir]`

**EXAMPLE**  
> `service lighthttpd start`<br />
`service lighthttpd stop`

or

> `/etc/init.d/lighthttpd start`<br />
`/etc/init.d/lighthttpd stop`
