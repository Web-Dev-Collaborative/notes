**NAME**  
lftp &#8211; Sophisticated file transfer program  
**SYNTAX**  
> `lftp [-d] [-e cmd] [-p port] [-u user[,pass]] [site]`

**EXAMPLE**  
> `lftp -u user,password ip`<br />
`lftp -f script_file`<br />
`lftp -c commands`<br />
`lftp --version`<br />
`lftp --help`
