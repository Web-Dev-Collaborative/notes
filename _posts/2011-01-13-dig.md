**NAME**  
DNS lookup utility.  
**SYNTAX**  
> `dig [@server] [-b address] [-c class] [-f filename] [-k filename] [-p port#] [-t type] [-x addr] [-y name:key] [-4] [-6] [name] [type] [class] [queryopt...]`<br />
`dig [global-queryopt...] [query...]`
`dig [-h]`<br />

**EXAMPLE**  
> `dig bilardi.net`
