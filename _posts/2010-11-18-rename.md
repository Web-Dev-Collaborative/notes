**NAME**  
rename &#8211; renames multiple files  
**SYNOPSIS**  
> `rename [ -v ] [ -n ] [ -f ] perlexpr [ files ]`  

**EXAMPLE**  
> `rename 's/\.JPG/\.jpg/' *.JPG`<br />
`rename 's/ //' *.JPG`<br />
`rename 'y/A-Z/a-z/' *`
